# ShopPilot AI - Software Requirements Specification (SRS)

## Version

1.0

## Product Name

ShopPilot AI

## Objective

Build an AI-powered voice assistant that helps small shop owners manage their entire business using simple voice commands in their local language.

---

# Target Users

- Kirana Stores
- Medical Shops
- Fancy Stores
- Garment Shops
- Bakeries
- Mobile Shops

---

# Core Problems

- Manual sales calculation
- Credit (Udhaari) tracking
- Stock management
- Profit calculation
- Daily reminders
- Expense tracking
- Supplier management

---

# MVP Features

## Sales

- Voice Sales Entry
- Daily Sales Report
- Monthly Sales Report

## Credit

- Voice Udhaari Entry
- Customer Balance
- Due Reminder

## Inventory

- Add Product
- Update Stock
- Low Stock Alert

## Expenses

- Add Expense
- Expense Categories
- Daily Expense Report

## Reports

- Daily Profit
- Weekly Profit
- Monthly Profit

## AI Assistant

Examples:

- "Today's profit entha?"
- "Ramesh ki 500 appu ivvu"
- "Milk stock entha undhi?"
- "Tomorrow reminder petti"
