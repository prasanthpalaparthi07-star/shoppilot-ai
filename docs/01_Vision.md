# ShopPilot AI - Vision

## Mission

Build an AI Employee for every small business in India.

## Problem

Small shop owners spend a lot of time managing sales, stock, credit (udhaari), expenses, and daily reports manually.

## Solution

ShopPilot AI is a voice-first AI assistant that helps shop owners manage their business using simple voice commands in regional languages.

## Target Users

- Kirana Stores
- Medical Shops
- Fancy Stores
- Bakeries
- Garment Shops

## MVP Features

- Voice Sales Entry
- Voice Udhaari Entry
- Inventory Management
- Daily Sales Summary
- Profit Dashboard
- Low Stock Alerts
- Reminders

## Long-Term Vision

Become India's AI Operating System for Small Businesses.
